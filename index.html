Answers to inevitable questions

FAQ

Do I really have to dress 80s Beach?

Yes. The theme was ratified in the spreadsheet, and the spreadsheet is our constitution. Think Miami Vice lifeguard, off-duty Hulk Hogan, or a man who owns exactly one pair of sunglasses and they're heart-shaped. Effort will be noticed. So will its absence.

What does "Don't Die" mean in the motto?

It's a legally non-binding safety guideline. Hydrate between beers, wear sunscreen, and do not attempt to prove anything about the ocean. The ocean has been undefeated for 4 billion years.

I don't golf. Am I useless on Friday?

Absolutely not — beach shift starts immediately. Someone has to hold down the house, ice the coolers, and heckle the golfers via group text.

How do I pay for my share?

Venmo the best man (Steve). Cost breakdown is on the spreadsheet. Bring extra cash for green fees and dinner. The groom pays for nothing — that's the whole point.

Can I bring a plus one?

Brother, this is a bachelor party.

What happens at the bachelor party...

...gets recorded on the scoreboard, because tokens must be counted. Everything else stays in Wilmington.
